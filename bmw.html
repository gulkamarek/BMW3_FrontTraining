7 Generations

The BMW 3 Series is a compact executive car manufactured by the German automaker BMW since May 1975. It is the successor to the 02 Series and has been produced in seven different generations.

BMW Motorsport

Subsidiary of German car manufacturer, was initially created to facilitate BMW’s racing program, which was very successful in the 1960s and 1970s. As time passed, BMW M began to supplement BMW's vehicles portfolio with specially modified higher trim models.

Alpina Burkard Bovensiepen

Company that develops and sells high-performance versions of BMW cars. Some Aplina models were assembled by hand at Alpina's facility in Buchloe but some are produced at the BMW assembly line.
